Blockchain Applications

Understanding the problems blockchain solves and the applications it enables.

Collaboration vs Cooperation Problems

To understand when blockchain is useful, we need to distinguish between two types of coordination:

Collaboration

People working together toward a shared goal. Trust is usually not an issue.

  • Team building a product
  • Friends planning a trip
  • Company departments coordinating
Blockchain: Usually not needed

Cooperation

Parties with different goals who need to transact. Trust is a real issue.

  • Buying from a stranger online
  • Lending money to unknown borrowers
  • Verifying credentials without revealing data
Blockchain: Very useful!
Key Insight

Blockchain shines in cooperation problems where parties don't trust each other but need to transact. It replaces the need for intermediaries (banks, lawyers, escrow services) with cryptographic guarantees.

DeFi: Decentralized Finance

DeFi recreates financial services without intermediaries. On Solana, DeFi apps process millions of transactions daily with near-instant settlement.

Decentralized Exchanges (DEXs)

Trade tokens directly from your wallet without a company holding your funds. Examples: Jupiter, Raydium, Orca.

Lending Protocols

Lend your crypto to earn interest or borrow against your holdings. Smart contracts enforce repayment. Examples: Marginfi, Kamino.

Liquid Staking

Stake SOL to secure the network while receiving liquid tokens you can use in other DeFi apps. Examples: Marinade, Jito.

NFTs: Digital Ownership

NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) are unique digital assets that prove ownership on the blockchain. Unlike fungible tokens where each unit is identical, each NFT is distinct.

Digital Art & Collectibles

Artists can sell directly to collectors with built-in royalties on secondary sales. Provenance is permanently recorded.

Gaming Assets

Players truly own in-game items and can trade them freely. Assets can even move between games.

Membership & Access

NFTs can represent membership in communities, access to events, or software licenses.

Real-World Assets

Physical items like real estate, art, or luxury goods can be tokenized for easier trading and fractional ownership.

Solana NFT Standard

Solana uses the Metaplex standard for NFTs. Compressed NFTs (cNFTs) can reduce minting costs by 99.9%, enabling collections of millions of items at minimal cost.

DAOs: Decentralized Organizations

DAOs (Decentralized Autonomous Organizations) are internet-native organizations governed by smart contracts and token voting. They enable global coordination without traditional corporate structure.

Key properties of DAOs:

  • Transparent - All rules and treasury movements are on-chain
  • Permissionless - Anyone can participate by acquiring governance tokens
  • Global - No geographic restrictions on membership
  • Programmable - Rules enforced by code, not lawyers

Other Applications

Decentralized Social

Users own their social graph and content. No platform can ban or censor. Examples: Dialect, Gum.

Oracle Networks

Bring real-world data (prices, weather, sports scores) on-chain securely. Examples: Pyth, Switchboard.

Decentralized Compute

Distributed networks for AI inference, rendering, and other compute tasks. Examples: Render, io.net.

Physical Infrastructure (DePIN)

Incentivize building real-world infrastructure like wireless networks and sensors. Examples: Helium, Hivemapper.

When NOT to Use Blockchain

Blockchain isn't the answer to everything. It's probably not needed when:

  • All parties already trust each other
  • A centralized solution is more efficient and trust isn't an issue
  • Data needs to be private and never publicly verifiable
  • The application requires changing historical data
  • Speed matters more than decentralization (though Solana blurs this line!)

Summary

  • Blockchain solves cooperation problems where trust is needed
  • DeFi enables financial services without intermediaries
  • NFTs prove digital ownership and enable new creative economies
  • DAOs allow global coordination with transparent governance
  • Solana's speed enables payments that rival traditional systems
